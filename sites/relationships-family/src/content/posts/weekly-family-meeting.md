A short weekly family meeting cuts down chaos and builds connection. Keep it simple.

## Review the week ahead

Run through everyone's schedule so there are no surprises. A shared calendar helps.

## Celebrate wins

Start positive — each person shares one good thing from the week.

## Solve one thing

Pick a single recurring friction point (mornings, chores) and agree on one small fix.

## Plan something fun

End by choosing one thing to look forward to together. It keeps everyone coming back.
