Planning a party feels overwhelming until you break it into a timeline. Here's the countdown.

## 3–4 weeks out

Set the date, guest list, and budget. Send save-the-dates.

## 2 weeks out

Confirm food, drinks, and any rentals. Plan the menu and shopping list.

## 1 week out

Finalize the guest count, buy non-perishables, and prep decorations.

## Day before & day of

Cook what you can ahead, set up early, and keep a simple run-of-show so you can actually enjoy it.
