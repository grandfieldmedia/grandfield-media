Getting great results from AI is mostly about the prompt. Five habits that help.

## Give it a role

"You are an experienced editor..." sets the tone and expertise level of the answer.

## Add context

The more relevant detail you provide, the less the model has to guess.

## Specify the format

Ask for a table, a list, or a word count. You'll get output you can actually use.

## Show an example

One good example of what you want beats three paragraphs of description.

## Iterate

The first answer is a draft. Ask it to refine, shorten, or change tone.
