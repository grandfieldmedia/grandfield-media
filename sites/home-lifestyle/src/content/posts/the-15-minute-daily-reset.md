A tidy home isn't about big clean-ups — it's about a small daily reset. Here's the routine.

## Clear the flat surfaces

Counters, tables, entryway. Flat surfaces collect clutter fastest, so reset them first.

## One-touch tidying

Put things where they belong the first time. Handling an item twice is wasted effort.

## Reset for tomorrow

Set out what you'll need in the morning. A five-minute prep tonight saves a frantic start.

## Make it a habit

Same time every day — after dinner works well. Consistency beats intensity.
